Graphic Design


I've been doing graphic design since high school, all for just school purposes. I had hands-on experience with typography, logo design, tarpaulin layouts, user interfaces for websites, infographics, and many more. I am knowledgeable in using Powerpoint, Canva, and Photoshop for creating designs.
